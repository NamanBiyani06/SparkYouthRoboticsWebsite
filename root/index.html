About Us

We're Spark Youth Robotics Club. A FIRST Robotics Team based in the Ottawa Valley made of big dreamers. From participating in robotics competitions to hosting coding workshops, our goal is to create opportunities for all students to delve into robotics and learn new skills.

What is FIRST Robotics?

FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) is an international high school robotics competition. Each year, teams of high school students, coaches, and mentors work during a six-week period to build robots capable of competing in that year's game that weigh up to 57 kilograms. Robots complete tasks such as scoring balls into goals, placing inner tubes onto racks, hanging on bars, and balancing robots on balance beams. The game, along with the required set of tasks, changes annually.
